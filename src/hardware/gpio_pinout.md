# GPIO Pinout

### OLED Bonnet

```
3v3 Power                       -   1
BCM 2       (SDA)               -   3
BCM 3       (SCL)               -   5
BCM 4       (joystick center)   -   7
BCM 17      (joystick up)       -   11
BCM 27      (joystick left)     -   13
BCM 22      (joystick down)     -   15
BCM 23      (joystick right)    -   16
BCM 5       (button A)          -   29
BCM 6       (button B)          -   31
```
